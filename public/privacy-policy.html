Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

1. Information We Collect

When you use our authentication services (Google OAuth, LinkedIn OAuth, or email/password), we collect the following information:

  • Email address - Required for account creation and communication
  • Name - If provided by you or OAuth provider
  • Profile picture - If provided by OAuth provider (Google/LinkedIn)
  • Account information - Basic profile data from OAuth providers

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate and manage your account
  • Provide access to our training and consultancy services
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. OAuth Authentication

When you sign in using Google or LinkedIn OAuth:

  • We receive basic profile information from the OAuth provider
  • We store your email and name in our secure database
  • We do not store your OAuth provider password
  • You can revoke access at any time through your OAuth provider settings
  • We only access information you explicitly authorize

4. Data Storage and Security

We take your data security seriously. We implement:

  • Encrypted database connections - All data is transmitted securely
  • Hashed passwords - For email/password authentication, passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • Secure session management - JWT tokens for secure authentication
  • Industry-standard security practices - Regular security updates and monitoring

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction - Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion - Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent - Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Data portability - Request your data in a portable format

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services for authentication:

  • Google OAuth - For Google sign-in functionality
  • LinkedIn OAuth - For LinkedIn sign-in functionality

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use session cookies to maintain your login state. These cookies are essential for the authentication system to function and are automatically deleted when you log out.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
